THE GR@ZIA
FASHION EXPERIENCE

We launched Gr@zia Fashion in 2022, as a curated and managed marketplace with an endeavor to inspire consumers to make fashion and lifestyle choices that best suit them. We have a wide assortment of offerings, across price points, to cater to women, men and children of diverse demographics. Presently, Gr@zia Fashion houses 1,500+ brands and over 1.8 million products across five consumer segments: women, men, kids, tech and home. Within these consumer segments, we merchandise across several categories including western wear, Indian wear, lingerie, footwear, bags, jewelry, accessories, athleisure, home décor, bath, bed and kitchen in order to cater to the diverse consumer's journeys across our platform. We offer a mix of brands across established national brands, international brands, luxury brands, and emerging labels and designers.
